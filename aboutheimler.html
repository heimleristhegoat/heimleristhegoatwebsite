Who is Steve Heimler?

Steve Heimler, better known online as Heimler's History, is a history teacher/youtuber who makes educational videos about various Advanced Placement (AP) courses, specifically AP US History, World History, Government, and Macroeconomics. Heimler also grades essays for the national AP exam.

Heimler himself didn't orignally think his channel wasn't gonna grow as much as he had thought. According to Heimler's own words, "I found Youtube as a place to upload all my video files. I never expected it to grow into what it is now".

Mr.Heimler is a current teacher down in Gerogia and would love for you to get a five fast on your next AP exam.
